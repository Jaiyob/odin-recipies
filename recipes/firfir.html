Firfir

Firfir, torn injera soaked in a rich, spicy sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • 1-2 tbsp. berbere
  • 3 ounces tomato paste (it comes in 6 oz cans, so 1/2 can)
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4-3/4 cup water
  • minced ginger and garlic
  • 3-4 pieces injera, torn into pieces
  • 1-2 jalapenos or other hot green peppers, de-seeded and cut into thin strips salt

Steps

  1. Cook the onions for several minutes until they start to soften and turn translucent.
  2. Add the oil.
  3. When the oil is hot add the berbere and stir.
  4. After a few minutes, add the tomato paste and cook for several more minutes.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes, garlic, ginger, and a bit of salt, and cook on low-medium heat until the tomatoes are thoroughly cooked.
  6. Add the water and bring to a simmer. It should look like a really thick tomato soup at this point.
  7. Add the injera and stir, gently chopping the injera as you mix it in.
  8. Continue cooking until the injera has absorbed all of the liquid. Top it off with jalapenos
